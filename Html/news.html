Since 2005, our bakers have been committed to ensuring that the finest quality of product has been produced. Every item has been carefully created to ensure it is a delight to experience. Finest Flour Bakery is a bakery that will serve as a gateway for sydney commuters.

Our mission is to satisfy sydney consumers needs and wants with exceptional quality baked goods and treats to improve their day.

We have been serving our loyal customer the finest quality of baked goods since we opened in 2005. That same year John Feddersen had a vision, to create a bakery that serves as a gateway for sydney workers, so he established the finest flour bakery.
